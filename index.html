Whack A Mole!

As a miner and treasure seeker, you discover abundant mineral veins in a small village. However, the mine is plagued by moles and snakes.

Use the mouse clicking to whack a male mole to collect mine or treasure to get scores. More combos more scores! Never whack on a female mole who carries nothing, or you would lose some scores and your combos would become 0. Whack a snake before it bites you and reduces your HP. Game over once time up or HP reduce to 0. Try to collect as many mines as you can within the limited time and challenge yourself.
